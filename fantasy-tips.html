Fantasy Cricket Tips

Top 5 Fantasy Cricket Tips

  1. Research Player Form: Always check recent performances before selecting players. Look at last 5 matches statistics.
  2. Pitch Conditions Matter: Tailor your team based on whether the pitch favors batsmen or bowlers. Green tops help pacers while dry pitches favor spinners.
  3. Captain Selection is Key: Your captain earns 2x points - choose a consistent performer who is likely to play full match.
  4. Balance Your Team: Don't overload with batsmen or bowlers - maintain a good mix (typically 5-6 batsmen, 3-4 bowlers, 1-2 all-rounders).
  5. Follow Team News: Last-minute changes can impact player availability. Check playing XI 30 minutes before deadline.

Check our Strategy Guides for more in-depth analysis and advanced techniques.

Additional Pro Tips

  • Select wicket-keepers who bat in top order (they get more opportunities to score points)
  • Include at least one death bowler (they often get wickets at the end)
  • Consider all-rounders as they contribute with both bat and ball
  • Check head-to-head records between players and teams
  • Monitor weather conditions as rain can reduce overs and impact scoring
