Advanced Fantasy Cricket Strategies

1. The Balanced Team Approach

Allocate your budget wisely across batsmen, bowlers, all-rounders and wicketkeepers. A typical balanced team might include:

  • 4-5 Batsmen (including your captain)
  • 3-4 Bowlers (including at least one death bowler)
  • 1-2 All-rounders
  • 1 Wicketkeeper (preferably one who bats high in the order)

2. The Differential Picks Strategy

Selecting less popular players who have potential can give you an edge when they perform well, as fewer opponents will benefit from their points.

3. The Match-Up Strategy

Analyze specific player vs team matchups. Some batsmen perform exceptionally well against certain bowling attacks, while some bowlers have particular batsmen's numbers.
